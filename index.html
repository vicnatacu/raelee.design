Company Bio

Raelee.Designs strives to design UX friendly, appealing and easy to use websites.

We create responsive design websites for all screen types.
We work closely with clients to ensure their site provides a seemless User experience for your clients, and a site that meets, the needs of our client, and their business needs. We cater to the needs of Startups, and Small to Medium Businesses. We are passionate about providing the UX experience needed to make the Blockchain Technology accessable to all.
We, also assist clients, wanting assistance with designing their own website using a content management system like Wix or WordPress.
We will maintain and update your site, if a client wants this service.

Company Values

Raelee.Design encourages all members of staff to live by the Company Values and incorporate them into their life.

6 Company Values Across all aspects of Life

Connections & Family

Family are the people you surround yourself with, trusted connections/relationships where adding value is seoncd nature. Positive relationships with like minded people.

Relationships

Developing connecitons with people, to better understands their needs. Develop trust, and show intergrity. Having itegrity enables trust. Communications is key in any relationship, as through communication comes understanding. Communicate early and often. Keeping people informed is paramount to any process. After all, a quote of significance: "The Biggest Challenges in life, will never be solved by comfortable conversations".

Service to Others

Service to Others is what drives Raelee.Design everyday. How in, unexpected little ways you can make a difference to someone.

Contributions

A strongly held belief is the importance of giving back, pay if forward. Taking time out to share, your time, and/or knowledge, and skills. To find a way to give back to your community.

Achievement

We often judge ourselves most harshly. The challenge is to be kinder to yourself. Ask youreself, are you making headway/steps towards your goals across all spheres of life? Acknowledge your progress, no matter how small, or significant, in little ways that bring you happiness (non food). Politely acknowledge and hear the kind words of others, for they are not given lightly. So listen and believe in what others see in you. See something in their words. Try to see it in yourself, even if it's just a little. Think of fun ways you can acknowledge and recognise to yourself your progress.

Self Care

Raelee.Design believes in Self Care. A quote from Maxine Horne and the Title a book written by Madoona King, "Think Smart, Run Hard". We believe, run hard, but think smart. Ensure you give back to yourself. We believe in "Living your Optimal Day", everyday. Little things you can incorporate in your day to recharge your battery. Raelee.Design encourages, good sleep, 7-8 hours, every night, exercise (you enjoy), meditation, a "No Screen", outdoor if possible, 30-45 minute lunch. Enjoy the outdoors in a shaded spot, enjoy the coming and goings of life. Read a professional development book. Wind down before bed, mentally unpack your day, prepare for the next. This then, leads to restful sleep when you empty your mind of worries. Love what you do. Live your values. Encourage and inspire others and add value, in Business and in life.
